Bio

I grew up in Arlington, TX and graduated high school back in 2010. From Fall 2010 until now I have been attending The University of Texas at Austin. I originally wanted to be an astrophysicist so I started out majoring in physics and astronomy but after my sophomore year I decided it wasn't for me. My biggest passion is (and has always been) problem solving, so naturally I was drawn to computer science. I had done some programming in the past but nothing too substantial. After my first programming class, I knew I had chosen the right major. What I love about computer science is the challanging aspect of it.
